Triangle inequalities theorem #

(1) \(-|a| \leq a \leq |a|\)

(2) \(|a+b| \leq |a| + |b|\)

(3) \(|a-b| \leq |a| + |b|\)

(4) \(||a|-|b|| \leq |a-b|\)

Special inequalities #

(1) \(-|\theta| \leq sin\theta \leq |\theta|\)

(2) \(-|\theta| \leq 1 - cos \theta \leq |\theta|\)
